## Discord Community

Our [Discord server](https://discord.com/invite/GnZtVZHAW6) is the best place to:

- **Raise a support ticket** - use the `#support` channel to get help from the team
- **Ask questions** - get answers from other Drift users and the core team
- **Share what you're building** - show off your simulations and get feedback
- **Follow updates** - be the first to know about new releases and features

[Join the Drift Discord →](https://discord.com/invite/GnZtVZHAW6)

## Email Support

For bug reports, billing questions, or anything you'd prefer to keep private, email us at [support@godrift.ai](mailto:support@godrift.ai).

When reporting a bug, it helps to include:
- Your Drift CLI version (`/version` inside Drift)
- Your Ubuntu and ROS2 version
- A description of what you expected vs what happened
- Any error output from the terminal

## GitHub Issues

For confirmed bugs or feature requests on the CLI, you can also [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/godrift-ai/drift-releases/issues).
